When all else fails...

Description

You dried out the chicken. You cooked the curry down too far. It's Friday night and frankly you have nothing better to do. The weather is awful and you can't be bothered to go to the shop.

You know what to do.

Ingredients

  • Smart phone
  • Credit card
  • Just Eat app
  • Hunger
  • Apathy

Steps

  1. Get your phone out.
  2. Scroll through your local restaurants.
  3. Get bummed out because your favourite place isn't on there.
  4. Pick something you don't really want anyway and ignore the voice of reason in your head telling you that you're not really that hungry anyway and besides you have stuff to make a pasta.
  5. Order with minimal shame and regret: save that for later.
  6. Check the tracker every five minutes.
  7. Get your delivery as quickly as possible and don't make eye contact with the driver as you don't have any change in the house to tip with.
  8. Serve, and try not to weep.
